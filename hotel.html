Hotel Introduction / 酒店简介

Howard Johnson Leonora Plaza Shanghai is located in the center of Jinqiao Export Processing Zone, Pudong New Area, Shanghai. The hotel enjoys a convenient transportation network and is surrounded by various restaurants and commercial facilities. It provides modern and elegant accommodation, high-quality conference services, and comprehensive business facilities for conference participants. 上海丽昂豪生大酒店位于上海市浦东新区金桥出口加工区核心区域，交通便利，周边商业及餐饮配套完善。酒店拥有现代典雅的客房环境、完善的会议接待设施及高品质商务服务，可为参会代表提供舒适便捷的住宿体验。
